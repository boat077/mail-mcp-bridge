# Analyze Email

Quick analysis of a single email. Read metadata, summarize content, and list attachments.

**Use when**: You need to quickly understand what an email is about.

**What it does**:

- Shows sender, recipients, subject, date
- Summarizes email body
- Lists all attachments
- Detects if email is part of a thread

**Example**:

```text
User: /analyze-email
AI: Please provide the email Message-ID
User: <msg@example.com>
AI: [Displays email summary and attachment list]
```

**Related commands**:

- `/analyze-thread` - For complete email thread analysis
- `/analyze-attachments` - For deep attachment analysis
